Hi, I'm Divya!

I am a second year Computational Media student at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I am excited to learn more about the field of CM and get to know more about the opportunities this major has to offer. What I think is so fascinating about CM is that it combines the efficiency of computer science with the beauty of art and design. I strongly believe that CM will be a huge industry with a variety of applications!

In addition to my studies at Georgia Tech, I am a member of Alpha Chi Omega, one of the eight sororities located on campus. I hope to actively work alongside my peers to participate in activities that will further my education as well as take part in our philanthropy. AXO focuses on domestic violence awareness, so I hope to become more involved with those efforts, and perhaps use my CM education to build and provide resources that could be of use to DVA.

In this portfolio, I have included a few of the projects that I worked on this semester, and talked a little bit about each. These projects have been so fun to work on and have been great learning opportunities. Each of these projects helped me better understand how to work with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This class has taught me important media and design principles that I hope to learn more about and apply to future projects! I would love to continue to add more projects to this portfolio and take part in exciting work!
